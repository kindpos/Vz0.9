NICE

The system should feel calm and predictable, even during a Saturday dinner rush. KINDpos is designed to reduce stress, not amplify it. Failures surface clearly. Behavior is consistent. A POS should behave like a good coworker.

DEPENDABLE

When the internet goes down, your system keeps running. Orders still fire. Printers still work. Every action is recorded permanently in an immutable ledger that cannot be altered or lost. Connectivity enhances the system — it does not control it.

YOURS

Your data belongs to you. Your hardware is not locked to a vendor. Your workflows reflect how your restaurant actually operates, not how a software company thinks it should. KINDpos adapts to the restaurant — not the other way around.

Built for how restaurants actually work.

Local-first: Runs on hardware in your restaurant. Cloud is optional, never required.

Event-sourced: Every action is recorded as a permanent fact. Nothing is overwritten. Ghost orders and double charges become impossible.

Offline resilient: Designed to operate through failure, not collapse because of it.

Show your work: Every calculation is transparent. Plain English. No black boxes.
